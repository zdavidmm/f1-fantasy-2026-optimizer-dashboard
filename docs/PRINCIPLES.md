# Winning Paradigms (F1 Fantasy 2026)

## Constructors-first advantage
Constructors aggregate two drivers plus qualifying Q2/Q3 ladder bonuses and pitstop scoring. This makes constructor slots high-leverage. Prioritize stable top-tier constructors before filling marginal driver slots.

## Reliability / DNF-risk minimization as tax
Treat DNF risk as a negative expected value tax. Even high-upside picks lose value when failure probability rises. Safe strategy explicitly overweights reliability.

## Budget growth compounding
Early value growth increases future optionality. A lineup with slightly lower immediate points but better price trajectory can outperform over multiple rounds.

## Transfer discipline
Avoid unnecessary -10 penalties. Additional transfers are allowed only when net expected gain exceeds penalty with confidence.

## Chip usage principles
- Limitless: best on weekends with predictable pace tiers.
- Autopilot: best when captain choice is ambiguous (2x decision uncertainty).
- Wildcard: use for structural reshaping when budget/value regime changes.

## Pitstop excellence
Pitstop points directly impact constructor outcomes. Track consistently fast pit crews and fastest-pitstop likelihood.
